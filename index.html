Work With Passion

There might be days when your job requires that you make some form of sacrifice. For instance, it might mean that you attend an official event outside your city/state of residence to lend a helping hand. If there is no real passion for your job, this sacrifice will most likely leave you unhappy and make you feel used. The reverse is the case when you are passionate about your job and what you do. You take up those extra responsibilities with a pinch of salt. Your co-workers will see you as a reliable pillar they can always lean on when they need that extra shot of motivation or inspiration.
